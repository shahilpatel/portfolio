About Me

Shahil Patel


Hello. My name is Shahil Patel, and I am a sophomore at Dublin High School. My personal career goal is to become an economist for a major company. I am an avid skier and enjoy taking and editing photos in my free time. I plan to go to a 4-year college and receive a degree in economics, and hopefully receive a Masters Degree afterward. In high school, I plan to take Advanced Placement Statistics and Advanced Placement Microeconomics, since these classes would help me advance my knowledge in this career. I have an interest in economics, as I am fascinated in the stock market, and the way our economy works. Incorporating computer science into my career is an ambition for me, because I am interested in the ways computer and data science can relate to this career pathway.

The experience I have that relates to this career is having an adequate understanding of the stock market and how it works. I own shares of companies that are on the New York Stock Exchange, and regularly buy and trade shares. I also have good communication skills, as I referee teenage soccer as a weekend job. This job involves communicating with adults and children, and maintaining poise and focus as I officiate. I have implemented these skills in Model United Nations club, which involves a substantial amount of public speaking and confidence.

Some of the major accomplishments that I achieved in high school is maintaining a 3.95 cumulative grade point average and earning a 5 on the Advanced Placement Human Geography Exam. Achieving these feats, required hours of hardwork and dedication. I needed to manage my time in order to both complete the required courseload, and study the content that I did not comprehend fully.
